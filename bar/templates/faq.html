What is BAR?

BAR (Beverage & Auction Register) is an on-credit purchase system created by Martijn Luinstra for the Introductory Camp of 2013. It has been radically modified (completely rewritten) for the camp of 2014 and updated for later occations.

I think I broke it, what should I do?

Don't panic and follow these easy steps:
  1. Create backup system: grap a piece of paper and write down the name of the client and their purchases.
  2. Contact anyone in charge (or Martijn) and let them fix the problem.
